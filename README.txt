## About
2d top down shooter similar to dragon age fights with guns / frozen sysnapse and roguelike combination

## Controls
WASD: move
Mouse: look
Lmb: shoot
Rmb: walk to position
R: reload
Q: use left Gadget
E: use right Gadget
F: pick up item or swap Gadgets
Space: pause

## Dependencies
- SFML 2.0
- Thor 2.0
- yaml-cpp
- TGUI
